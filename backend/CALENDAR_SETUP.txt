For scheduled meetings to appear on your (HR) Google Calendar, you must:

1. Enable Google Calendar API
   - Go to: https://console.cloud.google.com
   - Your project → APIs & Services → Library
   - Search "Google Calendar API" → Enable

2. Add Calendar scopes to OAuth consent screen
   - APIs & Services → OAuth consent screen → Edit app
   - Under "Scopes" → Add or remove scopes
   - Find "Google Calendar API" and add:
     • "See, edit, share, and permanently delete all the calendars..." (or at least "View and edit events")
     • "See availability on your calendars" (or "View availability")
   - Save

3. Get a new token that includes Calendar
   - Delete the file: backend/token.json
   - Run run.bat again (or connect a form again)
   - When the browser opens, sign in and allow access to Calendar when asked
   - After that, new interviews will be created on your calendar and will be visible at calendar.google.com

If you get "invalid_scope" when connecting, you skipped step 2 — add the scopes in the OAuth consent screen, then delete token.json and try again.
